 Model Performance 
The XGBoost Regressor was evaluated using several metrics to assess its predictive accuracy and reliability. The model explained 80% of the variance in stroke mortality rates, with the following performance metrics: 

  • R² (Coefficient of Determination): 0.8 – Indicates that 80% of the variance in stroke mortality rates is explained by the model.
  • Mean Squared Error (MSE): 62.6 – Reflects the average squared difference between predicted and actual values.
  • Mean Absolute Error (MAE): 3.9 – Represents the average absolute difference between the predicted and actual values.
  • Median Absolute Error: 1.1 – Measures the median error between predictions and the actual data.
  • Variance: 0.8 – Reflects the model's ability to capture data variability.
